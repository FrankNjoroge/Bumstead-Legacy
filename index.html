Meet & Greet with the GOAT

Get ready to meet one of the most iconic figures in the world of bodybuilding - Chris Bumstead! Join us for an exclusive meet and greet event with the reigning 5X Mr. Olympia Men's Classic Physique champion.

About Chris

Chris Bumstead is the reigning Mr. Olympia Men's Classic Physique champion. CBUM (his alias) has risen to prominence as one of the top athletes in the world of bodybuilding. With his impressive physique, unwavering dedication, and charismatic personality, Chris has inspired countless individuals around the globe to pursue their fitness goals and embrace a healthy lifestyle.

What to expect

At this meet and greet event, you'll have the unique opportunity to meet Chris Bumstead in person, hear his inspiring journey firsthand, and learn valuable insights for achieving your fitness aspirations. Whether you're a seasoned bodybuilder or just starting your fitness journey, Chris's wisdom and expertise will leave you feeling motivated and empowered to reach new heights.
